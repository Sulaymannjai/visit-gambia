Visit Gambia

The Gambia is a country that sits on the tip of the western coast of Africa, thereby getting its name the tip of Africa. The Gambia is on the Atlantic coast and surrounded by the neighboring country of Senegal. The Gambia is known as The Smiling Coast of Africa. It boasts of diverse people, culture, and its flora and fauna. It is also a bird watching destination.
